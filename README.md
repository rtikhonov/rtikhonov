<details>
<summary><b>Nanofabrication Center, Quantum</b> — R&D Engineer &nbsp;·&nbsp; <i>October 2022 — August 2024 · Moscow</i></summary>

R&D Engineer working on superconducting quantum circuits.

- **Electromagnetic Modeling** — RF and EM simulations in Keysight ADS and Ansys HFSS to optimize circuit performance and reduce parasitics.
- **Circuit Topology & Layout Design** — Planar quantum circuit layouts optimized for coherence, coupling control, and scalability.
- **Flip-Chip Assembly** — High-precision flip-chip integration of superconducting quantum devices.
- **Process Development** — Refined fabrication workflows; analyzed experimental data to identify process improvements and performance drivers.
- **Data Analysis & Visualization** — Analysis pipelines and research visualizations in Python (pandas, Matplotlib, Plotly, Seaborn).

</details>

<details>

- Developed test programs and methodologies for IC reliability evaluation at wafer and packaged level.
- Ran WAT (Wafer Acceptance Testing) and PCM (Process Control Monitoring) on 300 mm wafers using automated Tokyo Electron probe stations.
- Designed and executed reliability and performance test plans across electrical and environmental conditions.
- Analyzed large-scale measurement datasets and produced technical reports supporting process optimization and yield improvement.
- Statistical analysis and visualization in Python (pandas, Matplotlib, Plotly, Seaborn) and JMP.
